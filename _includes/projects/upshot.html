Upshot

A fun, realtime, automatic photo & video sharing Mobile app for events and group nights out

When it became clear Togethera wouldnâ€™t reach the critical mass required to continue, our team decided to utilize the technology which we had developed for Togethera to build Upshot.

Upshot solved the problem of groups of friends hassling each other the day after a get-together for the photos. Your group switched live-streaming on at the party and closed the app.

All the photos & videos taken with your normal camera app were then automatically synced with the group in the background. This was an experience similar to Snapchat but for groups.

As with Togethera, I was responsible for building the Android mobile app. The Apple Editorial Team featured Upshot under 'Editor's choice' globally at launch.

Key Features

  • Automatic background detection and uploading of new photos and videos taken using any camera app
  • Time based activation to only capture events and nights out
  • Auto content deletion after preset time (7 days)
  • Hundreds of thousands photos; shared, liked and remixed
  • Featured by Techcrunch
  • Featured multiple times by Apple & Google alongside apps such as Instagram and Snapchat
