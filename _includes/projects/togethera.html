Togethera

A privacy, family focussed social network for Web and Mobile

Togethera, a private social network for families. It was designed to be simple enough for kids, grandparents and your least tech savvy relatives to create a timeline of cherished family memories. Togethera brought thousands of families around the world closer together before shutting down in 2016.

I was responsible for building the Android app (Native Java) as well as helping out with the Web app (AngularJS) and Backend services (Python). Apple and Google featured our apps dozens of times over the years, but the best recognition came from the reviews and messages of thanks from families whose lives Togethera touched.
